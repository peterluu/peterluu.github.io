About Me

My name is Peter Luu and I'm a freelance web developer. Basically, I make things work on the web. I spend my time breaking things and trying to fix them again, hoping to make them better in the process. It all started when I blew up my first computer at 15. Nowadays, I try not to blow things up, but make them better than they were before.

I started building websites just for fun during my high school years, and guess what, I still like building websites just for fun now! But I especially love building websites about things that matter. I love helping small businesses make themselves known. I love helping charities and non-for-profit organisations get the message out about their causes.

At the moment, I'm based in Toowoomba, Queensland Australia. However, I'll be relocating to Brisbane, Queensland Australia in 2014. If you'd like to get in touch with me, fill out the form below.
