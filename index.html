About Me

My name is Peter Luu and I'm a freelance web developer. Basically, I make things work on the web. I spend my time breaking things and trying to fix them again, hoping to make them better in the process. It all started when I blew up my first computer at 15. Nowadays, I try not to blow things up, but make them better than they were before.

I started building websites just for fun during my high school years, and guess what, I still like building websites just for fun now! As of 2014, I am no longer offering my services as I am now committed to a full-time job. However, I may have some time for small projects if you ask me nicely in the contact form below.
